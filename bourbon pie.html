Kentucky Bourbon Pie

Dottie Nivens

Not only are Fannie Flagg's Redbird Christmas characters warm wise and funny; they can cook too!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup light corn syrup
  • ½ cup butter or margarine
  • 4 eggs, lightly beaten
  • ¼ cup bourbon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup pecan pieces
  • 1 unbaked pastry shell fitted into a 9-inch deep-dish pie plate

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees
  2. Combine first three ingredients in asmall sauce pan and cook over medium heat.
  3. Stir constantly until butter melts and sugar dissolves.
  4. Cool slightly
  5. Beat eggs, bourbon, vanilla, and salt in a large bowl.
  6. Gradually add sugar mixture, beating well with a wire whisk
  7. Stir in chocolate chips and pecans
  8. Pour into pastry shell
  9. Bake for 50 to 55 minutes or until set
  10. Serve warm or chilled
